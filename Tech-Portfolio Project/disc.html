Disc/Belt & Spindle Sander

The purpose of a disc/belt or spindle sander is to SMOOTH A STRAIGHT OR CURVED CUT.

1) Always use appropriate eye protection.

2) Don't use the abrasive disc/belt/spindle if it is torn or damaged. Report it to the instructor if it is damaged.

3) The material MUST be held flat against the table and hands kept clear of the abrasive disc/belt/spindle.

4) ALL adjustments to the machine are made BEFORE starting it.

5) Always check to make sure the table is adjusted for the angle that you want. Use a square to do this.

6) Work must be done on the side of the disc rotating downward. The LEFT side

7) On the spindle sander, sand against the direction the spindle is spinning.

8) Do not leave the machine until it has come to a full stop.

9) Don't use metal on the disc, belt sander or spindle sander. Sparks can get into the dust extraction and start a fire.

10) One operator on a disc/belt sander combination at a time.
