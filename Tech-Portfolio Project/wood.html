Wood Lathe

The purpose of the lathe is to TURN MATERIAL ROUND (bowls and spindles).

1) Have the instructor check the set up before using the Lathe.

2) A Face Shield must be used while operating the lathe.

3) Tie back LONG HAIR, remove loose clothing and jewelry when operating the Wood Lathe. Not doing so is extremely dangerous.

4) Check and make sure the TAIL STOCK is locked securely in place.

5) Follow gluing instructions and allow all glued work to fully dry before turning.

6) Check wood to be turned. DO NOT turn pieces with large cracks or large knots.

7) After mounting rough stock on the lathe, turn the piece by hand to see that it will clear the tool rest.

8) Start the lathe at the slowest speed then increase to the desired speed. NEVER turn large objects at high speed.

9) Hold all Lathe tools with both hands and a firm grip.

10) Keep lathe tools sharp, and report to the instructor when they become dull.

11) ALWAYS stop the lathe to make measurements with calipers.

12) Keep the tool rest as close to the work as possible, and NEVER adjust the tool rest while the lathe is turning.

13) When sanding and finishing on the lathe, ALWAYS remove the tool rest.

14) Use a SMALL CLOTH ONLY when finishing; fold it into a small pad to apply finishes on the lathe. NEVER WRAP THE CLOTHE AROUND YOUR HAND.

15) Check frequently to make sure that the wood does not become loosewhile in the wood lathe
