Scroll Saw

The purpose of the scroll saw it to CUT INTRICATE SHAPES out of wood/plastic.

1) Eye protection must be worn while operating the Scroll Saw.

2) Complete attention must be directed toward workbeing done, NO TALKING.

3) Keep your fingers clear of the blade.

4) Make ALL adjustments with the power off.

5) NEVER clear the table with your hands, use a piece of scrap wood or a brush.

6) Lower the HOLD-DOWN so that it presses lightly on the material being cut before starting the saw.

7) The thicker the wood, the faster the speed of the blade.

8) DO NOT saw stock that has an uneven surface.

9) Occasionally the blade will break while the machine is running. If this should occur, shut off the machine and notify the instructor immediately.

10) Hold the material firmly and feed it into the blade at a moderate speed.

11) Before backing out of a long cut, turn the power off.

12) Disconnect the machine from the power source when making repairs or changing blades.
